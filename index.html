Puffy Lux Performance

Recommended by Consumer Reports, the versatile construction of the Puffy Lux comes through with ‘Very Good' support for nearly every size and style of sleeper. We've tested the Lux in different sleep positions to determine the level of comfort. In addition to comfort, sleepers experience less back pain and feel relief with Puffy's body-adaptive Plush Dual Cloud Foam. We found the mattress to be extremely comfortable and provided great full-body support for side sleepers and back sleepers.

A luxury mattress without the markups, the Puffy Lux provides pressure relief, advanced cooling, and targeted support with Climate Comfort™ and Cooling Cloud™ foams. The perfect mix of soft and firm, the Puffy Lux's medium-plush feel truly worked numbers on nearly everyone that tried it.

Material

While a hybrid mattress isn't a new concept, Puffy added an ‘all-in-one' twist to it like no other. The Puffy Lux is made of 6 layers, from a hypoallergenic cover to gel-infused cooling foam layers and pocketed coils, it works on every level to improve your sleep experience.

The foams used in the mattress are CertiPUR-US® Certified, which means they're free of harmful chemicals.

Favorite Things about Puffy

- The cooling technology and optimized airflow wick away moisture and prevent night sweats. We would recommend this mattress to hot sleepers.

- The high quality of the mattress offers a soft bounce with body-conforming comfort.

- The bed is ideal for side, back, and combination sleepers.

- The happy medium that this bed reaches in firmness pleased almost all of us.

- We love that Puffy donates to multiple children's shelters and view it as an important part of their brand identity.

Trial, Warranty, and Shipping

Like most of the best bed-in-a-box brands, Puffy offers a 101-night sleep trial with all of its mattresses (though we don't think you'll need it!). If it's not a fit, you can return it for free and get a full refund. Additionally, a generous lifetime warranty builds a lot of trust between sleepers and Puffy. If there are any manufacturing or workmanship defects, Puffy will repair or replace the mattress for free.

All in all, it's just important to read the full warranty on this one. Unlike most mattress brands, shipping isn't free either. You can end up paying $200 for delivery and setup or $90 for UPS delivery and assemble it yourself.

Puffy Mattress Unboxing

Sleep on it the same night! Puffy mattresses are super easy and quick to unbox. This bed-in-a-box will need a few hours for decompressing and off-gassing but you won't need to air it out before sleeping on it.

Awards for Puffy Mattress

Besides being a product officially recommended by Consumer Reports, the Puffy Lux Hybrid has topped the list of best mattresses by People, CNET, Forbes, and numerous others. After running our tests, we agree with their assessments regarding full-body support, back pain relief, and cooler sleep.

Final Verdict on Puffy Lux

If you're searching for that top-of-the-line mattress without breaking the bank, there is no better option than the Puffy Lux. With its one-of-a-kind design, durable support, and exclusive contour layers, the Lux takes everything that is great about the traditional hybrid mattress to the next level.

Sleep Number 360® i8 Performance

There are few names with as much weight as Sleep Number when it comes to premium mattresses. The Sleep Number 360® i8 is one of five mattresses in Sleep Number's Smart Bed Series and possibly the softest. It is an adjustable air bed that adapts to your every movement, but the comfort it provides is outstanding.

The i8 comes with SleepIQ® technology, which uses sensors inside the bed to track your breathing, heart rate, and movements. Some of the bed's most prominent downsides include it being vulnerable to tech defects and how it handles our furry friends. Lightweight animals, like cats, won't be much trouble but heavier animals like dogs can disrupt the sensor.

Material

The Sleep Number 360® i8 is more or less an air mattress, albeit the most technically advanced air mattress we have ever slept on. The mattress is made of a blend of polyester/polypropylene foam layers, which are both soft and breathable. It includes air chambers under the mattress surface that is expandable and adapts to meet your specific needs at pressure points.

Favorite Things about Sleep Number 360® i8

- Different levels of comfort and firmness as the bed senses your movements. This was a strange but unique experience - we would recommend this for couples with different firmness preferences.

- Intuitive and smart interactions using SleepIQ® technology that provides interesting insights to sleep patterns.

- The i8 provides great back and spinal support, which makes it a great option for side and back sleepers.

Trial, Warranty, and Shipping

There's a 100-night sleep trial, but this is only for the mattress, not the bed frame. If you purchase a frame with the mattress - you can only return the mattress, not the frame. It also comes with a 15-year limited warranty. The first two years cover repairs, but wear and tear are not covered and after that coverage depends on the issue.

All in all, it's just important to read the full warranty on this one. Unlike most mattress brands, shipping isn't free either. You can end up paying $200 for delivery and setup or $90 for UPS delivery and assemble it yourself.

Sleep Number 360® i8 Unboxing

Being such an intricate piece of technology, the Sleep Number 360® i8 is not something you can just open from a box, watch it unroll, and forget about. Delivering and setting up the bed costs $200, which is a luxury tag you won't find with the other beds on this list. If you decide on setting it up yourself, it can take a few hours and you may need troubleshooting.

Awards for Sleep Number 360® i8

Sleep Number is no stranger to receiving rewards, and the Sleep Number 360® i8 is no different. Earning the editor's choice award from PC Mag and recognition from Insider and more, this is definitely a very well acclaimed mattress, if not a little expensive.

Final Verdict on Sleep Number 360® i8

The Sleep Number 360® i8 is in a league of its own for a reason. With an incredible design and impressive technology behind it, the Sleep Number earns most, if not all, of its price tag - but the maintenance is something worth considering.

Purple Premier 4 Performance

The Purple Hybrid Premier 4 is the latest model of the Purple mattresses. It performs exactly as you would expect a luxury mattress to perform, which is the bare minimum considering the near $3,000 price tag. This mattress provides enough support for light and average-weight sleepers, but may not support heavy-weight sleepers properly.

The thick layer of GelFlex™ Grid is a marvel when it comes to comfort and it does a good job at pressure relief, especially with back and shoulder pain.

Material

The Purple Premier 4 touts the signature purple GelFlex™ grid; well known for developing and passing the egg test. The Premier 4 features the thickest grid in all of the Puffy mattresses. Other layers include transition foam and responsive support coils.

Favorite Things about Purple

- We loved the responsiveness and pressure relief of this mattress. If you suffer from pain, this is a great option.

- The gel foam top layer and stainless steel coils team up for a really comfortable experience.

- This mattress sleeps really cool, thanks to the breathable polymer grid.

Trial, Warranty, and Shipping

This mattress comes with a 100-night trial period. You can return it after testing it for a minimum of 21 days and before the end of the trial to receive a full refund. Paired with a limited 10-year warranty, you can expect that this mattress might be swapped for a much newer model later down the line. Free shipping & returns are also included

Purple Premier 4 Unboxing

The high price tag does come with its perks, however. While the flagship Purple Mattress is manageable and easy to carry, the Premier 4 is the opposite. Luckily Purple provides a white-glove delivery and unboxing experience.

We recommend unboxing the Purple mattress and waiting at least 24 hours before sleeping on it for off-gassing and to avoid trapped odors from decompression. However, you don't need to decompress it in a separate room and move this impressively sized mattress yourself when it's ready.

Awards for Purple Premier 4

While the rewards have yet to pour in for the Purple Premier 4, it is no stranger to a great deal of top 10 lists across the internet and other publications.

Final Verdict on Purple Premier 4

While the Purple Premier 4 is an excellent mattress with a great mix of softness and firmness due to its GelFlex™ layer, it is not without its downsides. With its high price tag and limited warranty, it can be a little difficult to justify the price, even if the mattress is quite impressive.

Amerisleep AS5 Performance

The AS5 model is considered the most luxurious mattress from the Amerisleep line. However, they've decided to take a different route with the AS5 and look to perfect the soft mattress for lighter sleepers everywhere. We've tested it and confirmed that the AS5 outsleeps every model of Amerisleep that has come before it if you like softer mattresses.

While we definitely have some fans of softer mattresses here, those that need a more supportive firmer mattress may feel the AS5 is just too soft. We found that the mattress adapted quite well to any changes in posture or position and the multiple foam levels certainly do their job.

Material

The AS5 is an all-foam mattress that has a comfort layer of 3 inches of memory foam, a 2-inch compression layer, a transition layer, then a base made of high-density poly foam for structure and support.

Favorite Things about Amerisleep AS5

- This mattress performs well under pressure and is great for side sleepers, as it allows for the shoulders and hips to sink in a bit.

- Experiencing the classic comfort of memory foam but with quick responsive support.

- If you're looking for the softest mattress and don't mind sinking in, this is for you.

Trial, Warranty, and Shipping

The Amerisleep AS5 comes with a 100-night trial period and a limited 20-year warranty. This mattress comes with free shipping and returns.

Amerisleep AS5 Unboxing

This bed-in-a-box needs an average of 24 to 48 hours of decompressing and off-gassing so be sure to air out your new mattress. There is a slight odor when unboxing but lucky enough, it airs out within the decompression time and doesn't linger for long.

Awards for Amerisleep AS5

With many awards from Forbes, Sleep Foundation, and CNET on the AS5's performance as a soft mattress, there is definitely enough to back up their claims to be the best plush mattress.

Final Verdict about Amerisleep AS5

So while the firm supporters will find little to love here, the soft mattress enthusiasts might just have hit the jackpot. Side sleepers and back sleepers looking to fall into their bed will find a lot of positives here, maybe even their perfect mattress.

Leesa Legend Performance

The aim of the Leesa Legend mattress appears to be to offer a higher quality of fast and easy bedding. With multiple memory foam layers and recycled materials, does the Legend meet those expectations? We found that the Leesa Legend does bring a lot to the table to warrant the luxury label and price tag.

The well-designed memory foam layers are ideal for light and average-weight sleepers in different positions but heavy sleepers may want to look for a firmer option. This mattress offers a decent level of comfort, especially to side sleepers with the lower zoned support layer. In terms of pressure relief, we would recommend this option for those with shoulder pain.

Material

The Leesa Legend mattress is a hybrid model sporting an interesting amount of thought put into the design of each foam layer. The eco-friendly mattress consists of recycled materials, organic cotton, and a merino wool cover. From hole punching the top foam layer for comfort to designing the lower levels to support side sleepers and other non-conventional sleepers, a lot is going on here.

Favorite Things about Leesa Legend

- The comfort is provided by the single-focus, multilayered foam portion of the mattress.

- Soft zoned support and pressure relief of the hips and shoulders for side sleepers with enough comfort that back sleepers will enjoy it as well.

- Firm enough that you don't feel trapped or swallowed by the mattress but still has a soft bounce and responsiveness.

Trial, Warranty, and Shipping

The Leesa Legend comes with a 100-day trial period and a limited 10-year warranty, which might seem like enough for the lifespan of a mattress for some - but it's a little disappointing when considering the price of this model. Leesa offers free and standard shipping with all of its mattresses.

Leesa Legend Unboxing

Like many other bed-in-a-box mattresses, the Leesa Legend requires 24 hours of a decompression period. Luckily, the familiar off-gassing smell dissipates much faster than other models that we have tested.

Awards for Leesa Legend

The Legend mattress from Leesa has received some well-earned awards for its support of side sleepers with the middle comfort zone.

Final Verdict about Leesa Legend

Overall the Leesa Legend does prove to be an extremely comfortable mattress for the side and back sleepers but we wouldn't recommend it for stomach sleepers. While we think that the price doesn't exactly match the quality, especially if you are a stomach sleeper, we do not believe you'll be disappointed if you try it out.
